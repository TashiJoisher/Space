Tips

1. Use career planning tools such as the Individual Development Plan to generate a list of potential professions
2. Google potential careers to gather more information
3. Follow people in academia, industry, and government on social media (Twitter, Facebook, etc.) to learn about available jobs, fields, and trends
4. “Never Leave an Empty Seat” at conferences and seminars, as this is a valuable time to network
5. Attend informational interviews to gain insight into the daily routine of the potential job
6. Join LinkedIn and other job sites to find open positions and view their required qualifications and skill sets
